The second is a full Health and Physical Education teacher position for the 2026–2027 school year at the same campus; as that lies outside your subject areas, I mention it only in the spirit of completeness, and with the observation that Sammamish is approximately thirty miles from Edmonds — within the stated radius but at its outer edge.

Edmonds School District maintains a secondary teacher pool for the 2025–26 school year, which allows qualified candidates to remain on active file for positions as they arise rather than applying position by position. This pool mechanism is particularly useful when one's certification timeline is still in progress. Entering the pool requires no additional commitment beyond the initial application and places your file before the district in advance of any openings that arise between now and the autumn hiring cycle. My recommendation: this is worth doing at your earliest convenience if you have not done so already.

The broader Seattle metropolitan market lists approximately 340 high school English teacher positions, per Glassdoor data from March 2026 — a figure indicating a reasonably healthy pipeline of openings across the region. ZipRecruiter places the salary range for these positions between $52,000 and $148,000 annually, a spread that reflects the wide variation between smaller and larger districts as well as FTE status and step placement on the relevant salary schedule. The Washington Education Association maintains active school district job pages at washingtonea.org.

Seattle Public Schools is currently accepting applications and advises checking the job board weekly, as postings rotate with some frequency. In the autumn cycle, SPS traditionally posts Teacher Pools by subject area for external candidates, requiring either current Washington State Teaching Certification or eligibility for certification by August. The direct recruitment page is seattleschools.org/departments/hr/careers/teacher-recruitment/. With 224 to 340 high school English positions active across the district's broader search area at any given time, the market is reasonably active for a strong candidate holding or in the process of obtaining certification.

I flag it in case the specific local schedule is of use in planning the week, rather than as a suggestion that Mrs. Lang requires any instruction in the matter of music and young children.

For outdoor family activity: Edmonds Marina Beach Park and Meadowdale Beach Park present well-maintained environments for toddler exploration, and this week's forecast of temperatures in the mid-seventies makes both destinations considerably more agreeable than they have been since October. The Marina Beach Park is convenient to the town centre and has the advantage of level ground; Meadowdale offers somewhat wilder shoreline terrain for the more adventurous young naturalist who is beginning to form strong opinions about rocks, tidal pools, and the comparative merits of sand versus grass as a surface for sitting.
